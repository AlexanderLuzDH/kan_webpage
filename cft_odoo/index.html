Common Questions

Q: How is revenue at risk calculated?

Risk = (unfulfilled order qty) × min(unfulfilled, overdue upstream) × order price. Capped at order total.

Q: What upstream sources are traced?

Purchase Orders (overdue, not received), Manufacturing Orders (overdue, not produced), BOM Components (shortages impacting finished products).

Q: What's the typical payback?

2–4 weeks. Most companies protect $300k–$1.5M annually. ROI breakeven at ~2 weeks for Professional tier.

Q: Can we use CFT with Odoo Community Edition?

Yes. CFT works with Odoo CE 17.0+. No Enterprise features required.
