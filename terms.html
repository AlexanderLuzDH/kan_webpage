Terms of Service

Effective: 2025-01-01

Definitions

  • Certified Decision: an output meeting your chosen accuracy target (e.g., 95% or 99%).
  • Abstention: an output withheld due to insufficient confidence; routed to review; not charged.
  • Coverage: fraction of requests that return certified decisions at the target.

Billing

You are billed per certified decision. Abstentions are free. Optional SLAs and support add‑ons are available.

Disclaimers

Medical triage is decision‑support only and not a substitute for professional judgment.
